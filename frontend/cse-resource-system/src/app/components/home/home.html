📚 CSE Resource Hub

A platform designed for Computer Science students to discover recommended books and learning resources for core subjects.

About This Platform

The CSE Resource Hub helps students easily access high-quality academic resources for subjects such as Data Structures, DBMS, Operating Systems, and Computer Networks.

The platform provides curated textbooks, tutorials and online resources that support students in strengthening their technical knowledge and preparing for exams and placements.
